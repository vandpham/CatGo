FAQs

Find answers to commonly asked questions about the parking owners' registration process,
renting out parking spaces, and payments.

To register as a parking owner, simply visit our website and click on the 'Register' button. Fill out the required information and submit the form. Once your registration is approved, you can start listing your parking spaces.

Renting out your parking space is easy. Just log in to your account, go to the 'Manage Listings' section, and create a new listing for your parking space. Provide all the necessary details, such as location, availability, and pricing. Once your listing is live, interested drivers can book your parking space.

We offer secure payment processing for all parking owners. Once a driver completes a booking and successfully parks in your space, we will transfer the payment to your designated bank account. You can easily track your earnings and payment history in your account dashboard.

If you have any further questions or need assistance, our support team is here to help. You can reach us by phone, email, or through the contact form on our website. We strive to provide prompt and helpful support to all our parking owners.
